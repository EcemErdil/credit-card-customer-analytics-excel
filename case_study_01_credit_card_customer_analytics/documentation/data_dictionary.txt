CLIENTNUM: Unique customer identifier
Attrition_Flag: Indicates whether the customer is classified as churned or existing
Customer_Age: Age of the customer
Gender: Customer gender
Dependent_count: Number of dependents
Education_Level: Customer education level
Marital_Status: Marital status of the customer
Income_Category: Income segment of the customer
Months_on_book: Total number of months the customer has been with the bank
Total_Relationship_Count: Number of bank products used by the customer
Months_Inactive_12_mon: Number of inactive months during the last 12 months
Contacts_Count_12_mon: Number of customer service contacts in the last 12 months
Credit_Limit: Assigned credit card limit
Total_Revolving_Bal: Revolving balance carried by the customer
Avg_Open_To_Buy: Remaining available credit amount
Total_Amt_Chng_Q4_Q1: Change in transaction amount between Q4 and Q1
Total_Trans_Amt: Total transaction amount
Total_Trans_Ct: Total transaction count
Total_Ct_Chng_Q4_Q1: Change in transaction count between Q4 and Q1
Avg_Utilization_Ratio: Credit utilization ratio
Age_Group: Derived age segmentation category
Tenure_Segment: Derived customer tenure segment
Utilization_Level: Derived credit utilization level
